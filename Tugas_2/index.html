About

FC Barcelona, also known as Barça, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, Catalan, German, and English footballers, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and pride.

Barça is one of the most successful football clubs in the world, having won numerous La Liga titles, UEFA Champions League trophies, and other major domestic and international honors. With a motto of "Més que un club" (More than a club), Barcelona is famous for its attacking style of football and producing world-class players through its youth academy, La Masia.
